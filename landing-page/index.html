About Us

“Our product introduces a fusion of nutrition and workout features into a single application, allowing users to experience the best of both worlds in managing their health and fitness goals.”

Features

  • Fitness Calendar

    Easily schedule workouts, plan meals, and coordinate wellness activities according to your preference and goals. Add your day-to-day activities as well to ensure that you get the most out of Revive with your schedule.

  • Workout Plan

    With a diverse array of pre-designed workout routines and customisable programs, users can select routines that best suit their preferences or let us recommend a plan based on the questionnaire you fill on your dashboard!

  • Personal Diet Plan

    Through advanced algorithms and user input, Revive generates meal plans that prioritise nutrient balance, portion control, and culinary variety. You can specify dietary restrictions, food allergies, and cultural preferences to ensure the recommended meal plans reflect your needs and tastes.

  • Community Support

    Connect, inspire and motivate others on their wellness journey in a vibrant and inclusive environment. Through community features such as forums, group chats, and social sharing, you can engage with like-minded individuals, share experiences, and seek guidance from peers and experts alike!

  • Activity Tracker

    The activity tracker offers you the opportunity to log in your workouts, track your sleep quality and duration, record your food intake, and monitor other key metrics relevant to your well-being. Through customisable reports, you can visualise your progress, and identify trends.

  • Find Supplements

    With an extensive database of reputable supplement brands and products, you can explore a diverse range of options tailored to your specific needs and goals. You can access detailed information about each supplement and purchase your supplements through Revive for a hassle-free experience.

  • Interval Timer

    A versatile tool designed to enhance your workout experience by facilitating structured interval training sessions. With customizable settings and intuitive controls, you can create and execute interval-based workouts tailored to your fitness goals and preferences!

  • Fitness Facility Locator

    Discover, explore, and engage with nearby fitness facilities, including gyms, workout studios, and recreational centres! You can search for fitness facilities based on location, amenities, class offerings, and schedules, empowering you to find the perfect atmosphere for your workout needs.
